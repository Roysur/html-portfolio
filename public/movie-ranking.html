My All-Time Favorite Movie List

My Top 3

The Ten Commandments

An historical epic film that depicts the life of Moses, the prophet who led the
Israelites out of slavery in Egypt and received the Ten Commandments from God.

Ben Hur

An historical epic film that tells the story of a Jewish prince who is imprisoned
by his adopted brother and seeks revenge while also finding faith in Christianity.

Oklahoma

A musical film set in the American frontier, telling the story of a love
triangle and the rivalry between farmers and cattlemen in the early 1900s.
